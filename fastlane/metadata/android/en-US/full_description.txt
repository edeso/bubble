<p>Open PDF, RAR, ZIP, 7Z, TAR files.<br>Display BMP, JPG, GIF, PNG, WebP and JP2/J2K images.</p><p>Based on Bubble v1.6.0 by Nazar Kanaev. Upgraded dependencies, enhancements as felt needed.</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/edeso/bubble2/releases">Changelog/Releases are available at Github</a>.</p><p><u>Features:</u></p><ul><li>Optimized for tablets</li><li>Supports CBZ/ZIP, CBR/RAR, 7Z/CB7, PDF and FOLDER comics</li><li>TAR/CBT and Brotli/Bzip2/Gzip/LZMA/XZ/Zstd compressed TAR files are supported too</li><li>Display BMP, JPG, GIF, PNG, WebP and JP2/J2K image files in archives/from folders</li><li>Advanced zoom and scaling modes</li><li>Comics/Manga reading mode</li><li>Library with automatic bookmarks</li><li>Simple file browser</li><li>Best served on Android 8.0(Oreo) or later</li><li>Still runs on Android 4.1(Jelly Bean)+ (no PDF, Brotli, XZ, Zstd support though)</li><li>Free, open-source, no ads, gluten-free</li></ul>
